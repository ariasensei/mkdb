About Me

Hi, I'm Maria Kristina Dimaunahan — a Business Systems Consultant, Web Developer, and Educator passionate about creating smart, efficient, and user-friendly digital solutions.

I specialize in building customized systems that help businesses and organizations streamline their operations, improve workflow, and transition smoothly into the digital space. From simple websites to more complex systems with databases and client-side functionality, I focus on solutions that are practical, scalable, and easy to use.

My work combines technology and strategy. I develop websites using WordPress, GoDaddy, and custom coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and databases) depending on what best fits the project. I also work closely with clients to understand their needs and translate them into clear, functional systems.

Beyond development, I am also involved in education and media coordination, where I apply creativity, structure, and communication skills in teaching, training, and content creation.

"I believe that good systems don't just look good — they make work simpler, faster, and more meaningful."
